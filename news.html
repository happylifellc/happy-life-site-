ODDS Issues New Guidance on Behavior Professional Reviews

November 6, 2025

ODDS announced updates to the Behavior Professional Review process effective January 2026. The changes aim to streamline approvals for behavior supports.

My take: This looks promising for providers. The reduced turnaround time will help programs maintain continuity for clients without long gaps in BSP authorization.
