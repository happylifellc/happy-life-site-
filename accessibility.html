Accessibility Statement

Happy Life Group Homes is committed to ensuring accessibility for all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to make our website and services accessible in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.

We regularly review our website for accessibility compliance and aim to provide content that can be used with screen readers and other assistive technologies.

If you encounter any barriers accessing content on our website, please contact us at info@happylifellc.org or call (503) 509-3703. We will make reasonable efforts to provide the information in an accessible format.

We value your feedback and welcome suggestions for improving accessibility across our programs and online resources.
