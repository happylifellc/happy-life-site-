Group Homes in Marion County, Oregon

Happy Life Group Homes provides high-quality group homes for adults with developmental disabilities throughout Marion County, Oregon. Our homes in Salem and other communities provide a comfortable environment, professional care, and a supportive atmosphere.

On this page, you'll find information about our services, who qualifies for residency, and how to get in touch with us for referrals and placements. We strive to serve individuals and families throughout Marion County, including Salem and neighboring towns.

Services We Provide

Our Marion County homes offer round-the-clock care, nursing oversight, and personalized support for residents. We coordinate with the Oregon Department of Developmental Disabilities Services and local county programs to ensure compliance and high standards.

Eligibility and Referrals

If you or a loved one is seeking a group home in Marion County, Oregon, please contact us to discuss eligibility and referral processes. We work closely with county case managers and service coordinators to find the best fit for each individual.
