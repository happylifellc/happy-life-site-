Non-Discrimination Policy

Happy Life Group Homes provides services without discrimination based on race, color, religion, national origin, gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, age, disability, marital status, or any other protected class under state or federal law.

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment that respects and celebrates diversity among residents, staff, and community partners.

Any individual who believes they have been discriminated against in our programs or services may contact us at info@happylifellc.org or (503) 509-3703. Complaints may also be filed with the Oregon Department of Human Services or the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Office for Civil Rights.
